New Conglomerate : Connery

We are a coalition of outfits out to achieve a collective system of communication and coordination within the New Conglomerate of Connery.

The primary objective of this coalition is to co-ordinate consistent live-server action between online outfits respectively in hopes of improving our faction's day-to-day fights during alerts.

How can I get my outfit to be a part of this coalition?

If you wish to be part of the coalition and your outfit is playing on the NC in Connery, hop into our teamspeak channel and seek an admin to get more details on the procedure.
